A little about me

I’m Christine! I live in Austin, TX and currentely immersing myself into the world of design. I’m eager to learn, create, and innovate with people to develop valuable experiences.

I remember the feeling of being enthralled with the power art had on humanity and the way it intertwines with culture while leaving lasting impressions. I was introduced to design when I took my first humanities class in high school where I developed a keen eye for form, function, and aesthetics in architecture and art. My interest in the arts doesn't stop at just the visual arts, but also includes music, culinary, and even martial arts. I appreciate the way art permeates through different mediums and am always looking for inspirations.

Non-traditionally, I started my career in risk consulting which looking back was a great experience to connect with others to solve client problems. My job was to speak to and gather data from the clients, analyze the data, and provide solutions for their concerns. Although, I enjoyed working with clients to understand their problems, I felt frustrated that I only provided solutions, but did not have the tools to create the solutions. I transitioned to a technology start-up in Austin, TX as a Program Manager where I learned how to work cross-functionally in a fast-paced environment. This brought me closer to my interests in the art of technology, but I was still lacking the ability to create something that I could be proud. It wasn't until I discovered designing a good user experience, that I could see how cohesive technology, art, and design worked together. I saw the potential growth and was inspired by this new art form.

Whether it is creating a pleasing layout or an intuitive form that can save time, I found myself excited to finally create solutions to problems that were meaningful and had intention. With many years of experience both in the business and the tech world, I can bring my ability to work with clients and understanding of technology to use design as a way to speak to and motivate people.
